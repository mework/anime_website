In the year 2384, humanity has expanded to the outer edges of the Andromeda Galaxy. Kaito, a young pilot from a forgotten mining colony, discovers a dormant sentient ship from a long-lost civilization. Together, they embark on a journey across the stars to uncover the truth behind the "Echoes"—mysterious signals that predict the end of all biological life. As dark forces from the Galactic Federation pursue them, Kaito must decide if saving the future is worth losing his past.
